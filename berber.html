Best African Restaurants in NYC

Berber New Ivoire B&B

Located at 35 Carmine St (the West Village), Berber Street Food provides both vegan and non-vegan options, and does not (despite its name) limit itself to just North African food. Some items to try are: Akara (v), Nigerian Suya (nv), Mafe pot (nv), and Jollof Rice (nv).
